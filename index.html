Stop Asian Hate

We must stand in solidarity against the unjust treatment and persecution against AAPI (Asian American and Pacific Islanders) by supporting each other and emphasizing that everyone is human. With these ideas in mind, we should educate ourselves, listen and discuss with fellow AAPI, and encourage others to do the same. This will allow us to end the normalization of acts of hate against people of color. Racism affects our communities in different ways, but we must remember that this is not just a fight for AAPI but a fight for everyone against racism.
